Solar Calculator

A primitive solar calculator that allows users to search any address and analyze the expected nominal power yielded from placing 150W solar panels over areas shaded by the users.

EasySeating

An opentable-inspired Restaurant search-and-reservation app built on Rails and React/Redux. The restaurant search feature incorporates trigram logic to allow for slight misspellings of restaurant names. Users can see all previous reservations and leave reviews only for restaurants where they have had reservations previously.
